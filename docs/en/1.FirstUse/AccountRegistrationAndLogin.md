## Account Registration and Login
After installing the software, double-click the desktop shortcut of the software to enter the MipMap Lite login interface.

**Registration Steps:**
- Click Register Now to enter the MipMap account creation page.
- Select your country or region
- Enter your email address and password
- Click Register
- Check your email inbox, open the verification email, and follow the link to complete account registration

**Software Login:**

Enter your email address and password, then click Log In to sign in. If you have forgotten your password, you can retrieve a verification code via your email address to log in.
You may check the boxes for "Remember Password" and "Auto Login" when signing in. Afterwards, the app will skip the login process automatically upon launch.

>Note: Only perform this operation on trusted devices to prevent account information leakage.
