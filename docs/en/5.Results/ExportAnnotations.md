## Export Annotations

![](/img/en-img/exportannotation.png)

Click ![](/img/en-img/exportannotation2.png) to export all annotations for the current task.

Choose the desired data format and coordinate system / vertical datum.

![](/img/en-img/exportannotation3.png)

By default, the export is saved as a ZIP archive; select a destination folder to save.

![](/img/en-img/exportannotation4.png)
