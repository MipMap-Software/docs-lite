## Line measure

![](/img/en-img/line.png)

### Steps

- Click the Line tool to draw a polyline on the result for measurement.
- Left-click to add vertices, right-click to undo the last vertex, and double-click the left mouse button to finish the polyline and complete the measurement. Press `Esc` to exit measurement mode.
- The left panel displays polyline measurement results: planar length, 3D length, minimum slope, maximum slope, minimum elevation, maximum elevation, and elevation difference.
- You can edit the polyline's label name and color.

### Definitions

- Planar Length: the total length of the polyline projected onto the XY (horizontal) plane.
- Spatial Length: the total length of the polyline in 3D (XYZ) space.
- Min / Max Slope: the minimum / maximum slope along the polyline.
- Min / Max Height: the minimum / maximum elevation along the polyline.
- Height Difference = maximum elevation - minimum elevation.
