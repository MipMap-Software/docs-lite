## Area measure

![](/img/en-img/polygon.png)

### Steps
- Click the Area tool, then draw a polygon on the result to measure.
- Left-click to add vertices, right-click to undo the last vertex, and double-click the left mouse button to finish the polygon and complete the measurement. Press `Esc` to exit measurement mode.
- The left panel displays measurement results: planar area, 3D surface area, planar perimeter, 3D perimeter, minimum elevation, maximum elevation, and elevation difference.
- You can edit the area's label name and color.

### Definitions
- Projected Area: the area of the polygon projected onto the XY plane.
- Surface Area: the area of the polygon in 3D (XYZ) space.
- Projected Perimeter: the perimeter of the polygon projected onto the XY plane.
- 3D Perimeter: the perimeter length of the polygon in 3D (XYZ) space.
- Min / Max Height: the minimum / maximum elevation within the polygon.
- Height Difference = maximum elevation - minimum elevation.
